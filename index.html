About Me

Welcome To Fitness With Babu

Hey there! I'm Babu, your go-to fitness buddy and master trainer at Mojo Fitness. With a passion for fitness, I bring over 20 years of expertise and motivation to make your fitness journey enjoyable and effective. Specializing in Powerlifting and Weightlifting for both men and women , we offer personalized training sessions tailored to the individual client, as well as regular training sessions for others.
While most gyms focus on strength and lifting classes, we go beyond that by providing a diverse range of options, including Yoga, Zumba, Functional Training, Group classes, and more. At Mojo Fitness, we believe in making a healthy lifestyle accessible to everyone. That's why we offer gym memberships at an incredibly affordable price . Join us on the path to the healthiest version of yourself, where fitness meets fun!
